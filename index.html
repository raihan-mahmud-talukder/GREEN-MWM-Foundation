About Us

GREEN MWM Foundation is a non-profit voluntary development organization established in 2022. GREEN MWM is starting its socio-economic development activities for rural poor community in different areas of Bangladesh with the help of UN organizations like UNDP, UNICEF, UNDRO, etc. Simultaneously, GREEN MWM is starting to increase its development activities through implementing various programs and projects sponsored by many international and national agencies and organizations. In the meantime, alongside medical waste management GREEN MWM is planning to implement a large number of development programs and projects in various major development sectors like aquaculture, rural water supply and sanitation, socio-economic development, rural enterprise formation and capital development, relief and rehabilitation, disaster management programs, income generation and self-employment, environment development and management, etc. The potential programs and projects in the above sectors are integrated aquaculture, intensive sanitation and hygiene promotion program, socio-economic development program, low-cost sanitation and awareness development, rural water supply, agriculture rehabilitation and minor irrigation, human resource development and skill development training, micro financing/participatory rural credit and savings program, social forestation, sustainable environment management program, etc.

Our Mission

The main mission of GREEN MWM is to ensure a proper and efficient medical waste management system for Bangladesh. We want to answer to the problems that the current medical waste management system has and provide better performing system to reduce the health risks that is caused by an improper medical waste management system. At First, we want to solve one of the alarming problems in the system which is poor segregation of medical wastage. To solve that we came up with Smart Dustbins with RFID. These Smart Dustbins will help us to ensure a much better segregation. Next, we want to combine the whole process of medical waste management system under one online platform. So that, all the HCEs that are working with us can have a hassle-free medical waste collection and transportation. HCEs can book their waste collection time slot when their majority of the Smart Dustbins are almost full. Our staffs can also access the online platform to see their work schedule so that they can see when they are assigned to do a specific task. After that, we want to arrange different programs on different areas to raise the awareness of a proper medical waste management system. We will be discussing about the consequences of having an improper system. We will be holding training programs to HCE workers so that they are able to use the Smart Dustbins in a proper way. By doing this, we want to ensure a healthy and better future for our upcoming generations.
